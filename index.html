Luxury Accommodations

The Duffdun House is located on a piece of land with a rich history in Manali. Passed down through generations, this land was once part of the historic area known as Duff Dunbar, which is what Manali was written as on maps in the olden days. Today, it is a stunning luxury property that offers a truly unique and luxurious experience. Located in one of the most prime locations in Manali, our property is close to both the town and Old Manali, yet still surrounded by serene deodar forests, providing peace and tranquility. Come experience the beauty and luxury of The Duffdun House, where rich history meets modern comfort.

Indoor and Outdoor Spaces

Whether you prefer a quiet corner for reading or a lively gathering with friends, we've got you covered. Our two common hallways with comfortable seating provide a welcoming space for socializing, while our indoor and outdoor dining areas are perfect for enjoying a meal with family or friends.
Take in the stunning views from our gazebo or enjoy the serenity of our two gardens, including a private deodar forest located behind the house. And for those chilly evenings, gather around our cozy fire pit to warm up and toast marshmallows."
