Explore our Amenities

All our guests get much more than just the beautiful room that they've booked

Home cooked food

Have a perfect start to your day by enjoying your morning tea and breakfast while lazing out in the sunny balcony or in our colorful indoor dining room.
All our delicious food is traditional, home-cooked and made with fresh and whenever possible, locally sourced ingredients.

Outdoor sitting areas

The garden is the perfect place to catch up with friends and family over coffee or just spend a nice lazy day reading a good book. One can enjoy the sun or relax under the cool shade of the walnut tree over here.

Indoor Common areas

Lots of balconies, open spaces and common areas to lounge around.

Work from home

We have free wi-fi so you can work uninterrupted from home. Need a quiet place away from the gang for a while in order to concentrate? We have plenty of common areas in the house where you can find peace.
